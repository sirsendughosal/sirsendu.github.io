About Me üî¨

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati under the supervision of Prof. P. K. Giri. My research lies in the domain of Experimental Condensed Matter Physics and Materials Science, with a strong focus on advanced Raman spectroscopic techniques such as Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS) and Tip-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (TERS). I have worked extensively on functional nanomaterial synthesis (via CVD), material characterization, and the fabrication of nanostructures for applications in metamaterials, photodetectors, photothermal-driven solar water desalination, and neuromorphic devices such as artificial synapses etc. My expertise is complemented by a strong command of theoretical simulations, including Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Finite Element Method (FEM), which I use to interpret and validate my experimental results.
